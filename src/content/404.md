# 404

---

**Page not found.** You've navigated to a route that doesn't exist. Much like a nil pointer, this leads nowhere.

---

### Possible reasons

- You followed a broken link
- You typed the URL by hand and made a typo
- The page has been moved or deleted
- The universe is deterministic and this was always going to happen

---

### Suggested fixes

- *Double-check* the URL
- *Go back* to [home](/) and start over
- *Accept* that not all paths resolve

---

Maybe try [home](/) — it definitely exists.
